JOHNNIE WALKER

OUR HISTORY

  • Enjoyed and embraced in over 180 countries, Johnnie Walker energises the pursuit of your richest life.
  • Johnnie Walker was born from experimentation. Our founder John Walker pushed the boundaries of what whisky could be. Not satisfied with the harsh, inconsistent scotch of his day.
  • He blended together different single malts to create consistent, complex and flavourful whiskies with the result that today every bottle masterfully blends the finest and rarest whiskies from the four corners of Scotland.
  • The unique blend has reached every part of the world, making Johnnie Walker the first-ever truly global brand.
  • First created in 1908, the Striding Man has become one of the world's most recognised brand icons and is symbolic of progress for all those who enjoy Johnnie Walker.
  • Diageo India is among the country’s leading beverage alcohol companies with an outstanding collection of premium brands.
  • Diageo is the mother company of Johnnie Walker.
